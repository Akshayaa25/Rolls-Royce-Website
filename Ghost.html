ILLUMINATED FROM WITHIN

In the dark of the night, the Illuminated Grille emits a divine glow, bestowing the car with a distinctive, 24-hour identity.

RADIANT BENEATH THE STARS

The Starlight Headliner is emphasised with an ephemeral show of shooting stars. The celestial scene is a rarefied vision of pure beauty.

CAPTIVATING IN ESSENCE

The high-gloss, Illuminated Fascia is a masterful display of mesmerising craftsmanship. When Ghost is in motion, the wordmark is enlivened by the brilliance of 850 three-dimensional stars. When Ghost is in repose, the star field dims, and the fascia seemingly disappears into the abyss of the night.

All-wheel drive establishes newfound versatility and on-road dynamism without compromising the Magic Carpet Ride.

The Bespoke Audio system is tailored to Ghost's specific dimensions, creating studio-quality sound within the cabin. And because the aluminium Architecture of Luxury has high acoustic impedance, external noise is all but eliminated.

Powered by the next generation 6.75-litre twin-turbocharged V12, delivering impressive torque at low RPM.

Responsive touchscreens on the front-central control display offer intuitive, expedient, and refined control.

Remain connected for professional or recreational purposes at all times with wireless charging, four USB-C ports, and Apple Car Play.
